<li><p>错误<br>  在python中使用selenium进行网页自动化&#x2F;爬虫操作时，执行下面语句</p>
  <pre><code class="hljs python"><span class="hljs-keyword">from</span> selenium <span class="hljs-keyword">import</span> webdriver

<p>  可能会报错：ValueError: Timeout value connect was ……, but it must be an int, float or None…..(&#x2F;- -)&#x2F;</p>
</li>
</ul>

<ul>

</li>
